Fencing Services

Whether you need privacy, security, or curb appeal — we install and repair fences that last. SOS Hauling offers expert fence work with fair pricing and honest communication, every step of the way.

We build and repair:

  • Wood Fences: Classic, durable, and customizable for privacy or style
  • Chain-Link Fences: Affordable, secure, and great for pets or property lines
  • Vinyl Fences: Low-maintenance and clean-looking with multiple color options
  • Gates & Enclosures: Functional, secure, and built to match your setup

We’ll help you choose the right material and design for your space. Then we handle everything from post digging to gate hanging — no subcontractors, just solid work you can count on.

Need a fence upgrade or emergency repair? Give us a call. We’re available 24/7 and ready to get it done right.
