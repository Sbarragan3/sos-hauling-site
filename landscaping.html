Landscaping Services

Whether you want to refresh your yard or completely transform your outdoor space, SOS Hauling delivers landscaping work that’s clean, efficient, and built to last. We do more than make it look nice — we make it functional, healthy, and easy to maintain.

Our landscaping services include:

  • Seasonal and one-time yard cleanups
  • Sod installation and turf removal
  • Mulching, weed barrier, and plant beds
  • Irrigation repairs and basic drip system installs
  • Trimming bushes, trees, and overgrowth
  • Grading and soil leveling

We show up ready to work and leave your yard looking better than we found it. Whether you’re prepping for an event, improving curb appeal, or building something from scratch — we’ll help bring your vision to life.
