Bobcat & Grading Services

From rough grading to final leveling, SOS Hauling provides skilled Bobcat work for landscaping, construction, and property improvements. Our operators know how to work tight spaces, uneven ground, and tricky access — no problem.

We use our Bobcat to handle:

  • Rough and finish grading
  • Trenching and light excavation
  • Spreading gravel, mulch, or dirt
  • Concrete and debris removal
  • Leveling pads for sheds, patios, and driveways

We take care to protect existing landscaping and hardscaping, and we clean up everything after the work is done. Fast, friendly, and efficient — that’s how we operate.
