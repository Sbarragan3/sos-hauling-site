Handyman Services

Some jobs are too small for a contractor but too big (or too annoying) to ignore — that’s where we come in. SOS Hauling offers reliable, affordable handyman services for everyday fixes and maintenance work across Santa Barbara, Ventura, and LA Counties.

We can help with:

  • Minor drywall patching and painting
  • Fence and gate repairs
  • Door adjustments, locks, and hardware
  • Hanging shelves, mirrors, and fixtures
  • Yard and shed cleanouts, minor assembly

We show up on time, keep you in the loop, and get the job done right. No need to chase us or wonder what’s happening — we work clean, communicate clearly, and always make sure you’re satisfied before we leave.

Need something fixed around the house? Give us a call. We’re here 24/7 and happy to help.
