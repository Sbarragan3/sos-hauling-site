Our Services

We offer honest, hardworking help for any project — big or small. SOS Hauling proudly serves Santa Barbara, Ventura, and Los Angeles Counties with these core services:

  • Hauling: Junk removal, yard waste, and bulky item pickup
  • Demolition: Sheds, decks, concrete patios, and more — safely and efficiently
  • Fencing: Wood, chain-link, and vinyl fence installation or repair
  • Landscaping: Yard cleanup, planting, sod, mulch, irrigation, and trimming
  • Handyman: Minor repairs, maintenance, and general fix-it jobs
  • Bobcat Services: Grading, trenching, moving dirt and materials

Need something not listed here? Just ask. We’ll let you know if we can help — and if not, we’ll try to point you in the right direction.
