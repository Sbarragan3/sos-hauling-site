About SOS Hauling

SOS Hauling started with a dream, a borrowed truck, and a drive to do things right. Over 20 years later, we’re still showing up — with our own gear, our own crew, and the same hard work and honesty that built this business from the ground up.

We’re family-owned, community-based, and proud of the reputation we’ve earned. Most of our work comes from repeat clients and referrals — folks who know we show up on time, get the job done, and keep our word.

We specialize in hauling, demolition, fencing, landscaping, handyman help, and Bobcat work. But more than that, we specialize in being reliable — the kind of crew you can trust to handle your property with care, work hard, and keep you in the loop the whole way through.

We’re not just a service — we’re your neighbors. And we’re here whenever you need a hand.
