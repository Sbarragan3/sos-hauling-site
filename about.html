About SOS Hauling

SOS Hauling began over 20 years ago with nothing but a dream and a borrowed truck. What started as a side hustle has grown into a trusted, community-rooted business proudly serving Santa Barbara, Ventura, and Los Angeles Counties.

We’re not a franchise — we’re family. We believe in showing up, working hard, and treating every customer like a neighbor. Because around here, you probably are.

Whether it’s hauling, demolition, landscaping, or handyman work, we take pride in doing the job right — every time. No shortcuts. Just honest work and open communication from start to finish.

We’re grateful for the trust our community has placed in us, and we’re excited to keep growing, one project at a time.
