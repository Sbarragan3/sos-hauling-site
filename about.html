About SOS Hauling

SOS Hauling began over 20 years ago with nothing but a dream and a borrowed truck. Today, we’re proud to serve Santa Barbara, Ventura, and Los Angeles County with reliable, community-first hauling, demolition, fencing, landscaping, handyman, and bobcat services.

We're a family-driven business built on hard work, integrity, and open communication. Whether the job is big or small, we don’t stop until the customer is satisfied.

We treat every project with respect, and every client like a neighbor — because around here, you probably are.
